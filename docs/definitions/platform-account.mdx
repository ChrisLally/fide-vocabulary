## Summary

- **Hex Code:** `21`
- **Standard Alignment:** [`schema:OnlineAccount`](https://schema.org/OnlineAccount) (Close fit)

<Callout type="warning" title="Not">
  Authenticated by a host authority's database, not purely by mathematics.
</Callout>
