## Summary

- **Hex Code:** `40`
- **Standard Alignment:** [`schema:Place`](https://schema.org/Place) + [`schema:VirtualLocation`](https://schema.org/VirtualLocation) (Broad fit)

<Callout type="warning" title="Not">
  Not a happening or a physical artifact. Can an Actor have presence there? Is its identity a spatial address rather than a document location or account?
</Callout>

## Fide ID Rules

### As Entity Type

- MUST use [Reference Type](/fide-id/specification/index#reference-type) [`NetworkResource`](/vocabulary/definitions/network-resource).
